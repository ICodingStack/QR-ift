## 🎨 One-Sentence Description

Generate stunningly beautiful, fully customizable QR codes with glassmorphism design, particle effects, and premium styling — all in your browser, 100% free forever.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 🎨 **Artistic Customization**: Choose from 5 dot styles, 3 corner styles, gradients, and more
- 🌈 **Premium Design**: Glassmorphism UI with subtle animations and micro-interactions
- 🌓 **Dark/Light Mode**: Automatic theme switching with smooth transitions
- 🖼️ **Logo Integration**: Add built-in icons or upload your own logo with opacity control
- 📱 **Responsive**: Works flawlessly on mobile, tablet, and desktop
- 🔐 **100% Client-Side**: No data leaves your browser — completely private
- 🚫 **No Ads, No Tracking**: Just a beautiful tool, no strings attached
- 💾 **Local History**: Your recent QR codes saved locally for quick access
- 🎁 **Surprise Me**: One-click stylish presets for instant inspiration
- 📤 **Multiple Exports**: Download as PNG, SVG, copy to clipboard, or share directly

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Enter your content**: Type a URL, text, phone number, or any data into the input field
2. **Customize**: Adjust dot style, colors, gradients, corners, and add a logo
3. **Preview**: Watch your artistic QR code generate in real-time
4. **Export**: Download as PNG/SVG, copy to clipboard, or share directly
5. **Reuse**: Click any item in "Recent" to regenerate a previous QR

### Quick Presets
Click any preset button (🔗 URL, 📶 WiFi, 📞 Phone, etc.) to auto-fill common formats.

### Pro Tips
- Use **gradient mode** for eye-catching brand-aligned QR codes
- Add your **logo at 30% opacity** for subtle branding without breaking scannability
- Try **"Surprise Me"** for instant design inspiration
